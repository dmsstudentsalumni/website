About Diamond Model School Alumni Association (DMSAA)

The Diamond Model School Alumni Association (DMSAA) is a vibrant community of former students united by the shared values, education, and lifelong memories of our beloved alma mater.

Chartered on 25th July 2025, the association was established with the vision of bringing together alumni from different generations, professions, and walks of life. Though young in years, DMSAA has quickly grown into a strong network dedicated to fostering meaningful connections, supporting current students, and contributing to the growth of our school and community.

We are committed to upholding the Diamond Model spirit of excellence, integrity, and service, while creating opportunities for collaboration, mentorship, and giving back to the institution that shaped our lives.

Message from Our Chartered President

Prabhab Tiwari

Chartered President, DMSAA

Class of 2079 BS

"It is with profound honor and humility that I serve as the Chartered President of the Diamond Model School Alumni Association (DMSAA). On 25th July 2025, we marked a historic milestone as our association was officially chartered, giving birth to a new chapter in the journey of our beloved alma mater.

Our school has always been more than an institution—it has been a family that shaped our dreams, values, and character. As alumni, we now carry forward this legacy of excellence, unity, and service.

This chartered year is not just the beginning of an organization, but the start of a shared vision—to connect generations, mentor students, and contribute meaningfully to the continued growth of Diamond Model School.

Together, we will build a vibrant community where our bonds remain strong and our alma mater continues to shine as a beacon of knowledge and inspiration for years to come."

Prabhab Tiwari
