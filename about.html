Our Story

The journey of Diamond Model School Alumni Association

The Diamond Model School Alumni Association (DMSAA) was born from a shared desire to maintain the strong bonds formed during our school years and to give back to the institution that shaped us. Chartered on 25th July 2025, our association represents the collective voice of former students who carry the Diamond Model spirit into their communities and professions.

What began as informal gatherings of graduates has evolved into a structured organization with a clear mission: to foster lifelong connections among alumni, support current students, and contribute to the continued excellence of Diamond Model School.

Today, we are a growing network of professionals, innovators, and leaders across diverse fields, united by our common foundation and commitment to the values instilled in us during our formative years at Diamond Model School.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our association forward

Our Mission

To connect, engage, and support Diamond Model School alumni through meaningful programs, events, and initiatives that strengthen our community, foster professional growth, and contribute to the continued excellence of our alma mater.

Our Vision

To build a vibrant, global network of Diamond Model School alumni who are actively engaged in supporting each other, mentoring current students, and enhancing the reputation and legacy of our beloved institution for generations to come.

Our Journey

Key milestones in the development of our association

Though newly chartered, the roots of our association run deep. The idea for a formal alumni network had been discussed for years among graduates who recognized the potential of our collective strength.

In early 2025, a dedicated group of alumni from various graduating classes came together to establish the framework for what would become the official Diamond Model School Alumni Association. Through months of planning, drafting bylaws, and building support, we laid the groundwork for an organization that would stand the test of time.
